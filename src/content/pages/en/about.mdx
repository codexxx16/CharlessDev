I am Charless Netumbare, a Full Stack Developer and AI Engineer from Masvingo, Zimbabwe. I am passionate about building secure, scalable, and intelligent digital solutions from fintech ecosystems to AI-powered applications.

I started my programming journey in 2019, self-teaching Python, JavaScript, and web development through YouTube, documentation, and relentless experimentation. What began as curiosity quickly became a craft. I have since built production-grade systems handling real users and real money.

My work sits at the intersection of software engineering, artificial intelligence, and financial technology. I believe technology should solve real problems for real people, especially in underserved markets like Zimbabwe and the broader African continent.

Outside of code, I am a martial artist (Muay Thai and Boxing), a gamer, and someone who finds deep satisfaction in the discipline of both physical and mental training.
